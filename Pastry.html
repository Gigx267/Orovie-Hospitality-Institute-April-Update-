Essential Kit & Ingredients

Baking & Decorating Requirements

  • Plastic airtight bowls for icing storage
  • Apron & professional chef jacket
  • Toothpick set & small detailing tools
  • Scissors (straight & micro-tip)
  • Wax paper / parchment rounds
  • Piping bags (disposable & reusable)
  • Icing colors: paste (Wilton/Sugarflair) green, yellow, red, black etc.
  • Icing nozzles/tips: #3, #21/32, #352/67, #104/103, #12, #47/48, #5, #16/18, #224/225, #2D
  • Spatulas: one large, one small offset
  • Clown heads / decorative toppers & moulds
  • 6" dummy cake for practice tiers
  • Cake comb & decorating turn table
  • Fondant smoothers & rolling pins (smooth + embossed)
  • Glycerin, glucose syrup, gelatin, vegetable shortening
  • Blossom cutters, brandy extract, and edible lustre dusts
Starter toolkit available Premium nozzles recommended Fondant practice kit included

Our Signature Approach

From Beginner to Sugar Artist

We offer a unique workshop programme that covers all aspects of Cake Craft and Decoration — from basic sugarcraft classes where you can learn how to ice a cake successfully, to the advanced and hugely popular themed cakes. Our workshop categories focus on Cakes, Cupcakes, Cake Jewellery and Cake Toppers. Themes include 'Football Club Cakes', 'Professional Cakes', and 'Mini Christmas Cakes'.

Because our classes are suitable for the beginner through to the advanced sugar crafter, there is sure to be a course to suit you. We choose themes very carefully to inspire creativity and professionalism.
