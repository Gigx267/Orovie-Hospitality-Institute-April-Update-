The Architect of Prestige

Comprehensive Brand Management Curriculum

This programme equips students with the strategic frameworks and creative tools to build, manage, and elevate brands. From brand positioning and identity design to digital reputation management and crisis communication — graduates become brand guardians for luxury hotels, lifestyle labels, and hospitality enterprises.

BRM401
Foundations of Brand Management
Brand equity, brand architecture, positioning, and the psychology of consumer perception.
BRM402
Luxury Brand Strategy
Exclusivity, heritage, craftsmanship, and the unique dynamics of premium hospitality branding.
BRM403
Brand Identity & Visual Design
Logo development, colour psychology, typography, and creating a cohesive visual language.
BRM404
Digital Branding & Social Media Management
Content strategy, influencer partnerships, community management, and platform optimisation.
BRM405
Reputation Management & Crisis Communication
Monitoring brand sentiment, handling negative PR, and protecting brand integrity.
BRM406
Brand Storytelling & Content Creation
Crafting compelling narratives, brand voice, and omnichannel content strategies.
BRM407
Customer Experience & Brand Loyalty
Journey mapping, touchpoint optimisation, and building emotional connections with guests.
BRM408
Brand Analytics & Market Research
Measuring brand health, consumer insights, competitive analysis, and ROI of branding initiatives.
BRM409
Integrated Marketing Communications
Aligning advertising, PR, events, and digital channels under a unified brand voice.
BRM410
Brand Partnerships & Sponsorships
Strategic alliances, co-branding, and leveraging partnerships for brand growth.
BRM411
Internal Branding & Employee Advocacy
Building brand culture, training teams as brand ambassadors, and aligning operations with brand promise.
BRM412
Capstone: Brand Launch & Management Plan
Develop a complete brand strategy for a new hospitality concept or reposition an existing brand.
Programme Prerequisites

Eligibility & Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent (for diploma/degree)
  • Strong creative and strategic thinking abilities
  • Basic understanding of marketing principles (recommended)
  • Personal laptop with design or presentation software access
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Keen aesthetic sense and attention to detail
Diploma (12 months) Advanced Certificate (6 months) Bachelor's Degree (36 months)

Programme Highlights

Duration: 12-36 months depending on pathway
Practical immersion: Live brand audits, rebranding projects for real hospitality businesses, and portfolio development.
Unique features: Guest lectures from luxury brand directors, agency visits, and a capstone brand pitch to industry panel.
Tools mastered: Brand tracking software, social listening platforms, design tools (Canva, Adobe Creative Cloud basics).

Key Competencies Gained

  • ✓ Develop and execute comprehensive brand strategies
  • ✓ Manage brand identity and visual systems
  • ✓ Navigate brand crises with confidence
  • ✓ Measure and optimise brand performance
  • ✓ Lead brand teams and agency partners effectively
